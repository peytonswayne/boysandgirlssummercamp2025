Boys and Girls Summer Camp 2025

Join us for fun, games, and creativity from July 7–18!

Welcome!

Get ready for the best two weeks of your summer! Our amazing camp counselors are bringing the energy, creativity, and fun to a boys and girls summer camp like no other. From July 7th to July 18th, 9 a.m to 12 p.m, Boys and Girls aged 5-10. Can't wait to see you there!

Boys Camp

Led by Coach Sean and Coach P (Peyton), the Boys will play sports and games all day long. from soccer and basketball to relay races and capture the flag, it's all about action and teamwork.

Girls Camp

Guided by our fun-loving counselor Laney, the Girls Camp is a filled with painting, crafts, games, and laughter. It's all about creative, connective, and joyful fun!

About Us

Coach Sean

Sean is a senior at NC State University and co-leads the Boys Camp. He brings high energy, great sports knowledge, and a passion for helping kids have fun and build confidence.

Laney

Laney is a senior at NC State University and leads the Girls Camp. With a creative heart and a love for kids, she creates a joyful and welcoming environment full of art, fun, and friendship.

Coach Peyton

Peyton is a rising senior at the University of South Carolina and co-leads the Boys Camp alongside Sean. After successfully running the camp last year, he’s thrilled to be back creating awesome experiences for the kids!
